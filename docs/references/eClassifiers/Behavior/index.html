A consistent, characteristic pattern in how a persona operates. Behaviors are habitual, preference-based, and cross-situational.

They capture: - Attitudes (risk-averse, data-driven, collaborative) - Work style (async vs synchronous, detail-oriented, shortcut-taking) - Motivation drivers (speed, quality, confidence, accuracy) - Decision inclinations (consult peers, trust AI, check data first) - Interaction patterns (reads docs, prefers autocomplete, avoids manual steps)

Examples: - ‘Checks results multiple times before making a decision’ - ‘Prefers automation over manual review’

Behaviors help design how the experience should feel. They guide design principles.
