A fictional but research-based representation of a target user.

Purpose: to help the product team visualize who they are building for.

Includes demographics, behaviors, motivations, context (work environment, tools), and attitudes/preferences.

Example: ‘Alex, a 34-year-old DevOps engineer who values automation and hates manual workflows.’

Personas have needs, goals, and pain points. In this model they are contained by a persona for simplicity. Shared needs, goals, or pain points are addressed by reference subtypes or ‘related to’ references.
