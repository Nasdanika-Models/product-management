Subscriptions define how the user pays and for how long.

Examples: - Monthly vs yearly - Per-user vs per-device - Pay-as-you-go vs committed - Auto-renewal terms - Usage-based or hybrid pricing

A subscription describes pricing and time, not features. However, a subscription may also define service levels — for example, LLM models available, token budget, response time.
