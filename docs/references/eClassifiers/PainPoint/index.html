PainPoint

  • Inheritance

    Default Graph Circular Graph Force Graph Force Graph 3D
  • Load specification

The problem, frustration, or barrier preventing the user from meeting their goals.

Think: ‘What is getting in their way?’

Examples: - Release notes are scattered across tools - Manual steps cause errors - Slow CI pipeline delays deployments - They do not trust the monitoring alerts

Pain points are what your product should aim to resolve. Pain points violate needs and block goals.
